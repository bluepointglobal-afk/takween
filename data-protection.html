Data Protection

Last updated: April 2026 · Takween Technologies · تكوين تقنيات

Our Commitment

Takween Technologies handles sensitive infrastructure data for government bodies, international organisations, and enterprise clients. Data protection is not a compliance checkbox — it is a core operational requirement for every engagement we undertake.

Regulatory Framework

We operate in full compliance with:

  • Saudi Arabia Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and its implementing regulations
  • National Cybersecurity Authority (NCA) Essential Cybersecurity Controls (ECC)
  • Communications, Space & Technology Commission (CST) requirements
  • Contractual data sovereignty obligations with government and IOC-level clients

Data Sovereignty

For Saudi government and public-sector engagements, all data is processed and stored within Kingdom of Saudi Arabia boundaries unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing. We operate private Class C cloud infrastructure for sensitive deployments.

Security Controls

Our technical controls include end-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, audit logging, regular penetration testing, and incident response procedures aligned with NCA guidelines.

Sensitive Deployments

For mission-critical deployments (Haramain, Olympic venues, World Cup infrastructure), we operate under bespoke data handling agreements that govern data classification, retention, access, and destruction.
